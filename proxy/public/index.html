      Frequently Asked Questions

      For Violinists, By Violinists

      At Kennedy Violins we are a family of string players committed to helping fellow musicians reach their goals. We are here to answer your musical questions, help find the perfect instrument for your needs, and provide resources for learning the music you want to play. Give us a call and one of our friendly customer service representatives will be happy to assist!

      Please call 1-800-779-0242 to ask for any special requests with your order or email us at support@kennedyviolins.com
